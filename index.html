What is EmTech?

Emerging Technologies - EmTech for short - is a project from the State University of New York, SUNY, designed to provide a lifelong learning and success experience to faculty, students, and professionals of all kinds.

EmTech comprises two main tools: EmTech Wiki, where you will find hundreds of different tools and reviews according to your needs; and the EmTech MOOC, the perfect place for understanding lifelong learning and success.
